About company

A scooter is an ideal form of transport for an urban lifestyle. Ignore the traffic jams on the roads, moving economically and comfortably on the roads of your city.

Thanks to the location and infrastructure of the territory adjacent to the store, our visitors can take a test drive on any of the vehicles presented in the hall before making a purchase.

For a test drive of large equipment, a preliminary appointment is required.

Mobile two-wheeled transport is indispensable, because it allows you to avoid traffic jams and get to your destination as quickly as possible. The maneuverability of the scooter is one of its main advantages. This transport is inexpensive to maintain (which allows you to keep it even for people with small incomes), any consumables and spare parts are always on sale. Scooters are convenient to use for country trips (for example, to the forest or to the country).

When choosing, consider: - the volume and power of the engine (for beginners, it is better to take models with engines up to 50 cc. cm.); - the capacity of the fuel tank; - dimensions of the wheels, the design of the braking system, type of transmission; - load capacity, maximum speed; - weight, dimensions, design of the front and rear suspension; - complete set (the presence of a trunk, reflectors, and other accessories).

There are scooters designed to carry one or two people. New modern models are equipped with LED optics, which differ in the density and saturation of the luminous flux.
